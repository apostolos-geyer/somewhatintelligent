## Recurring authorial material

- Technical precision used as the setup for an indecent or self-implicating turn.
- Distrust of systems paired with a compulsion to build systems.
- Platforms as power, not neutral containers.
- Interfaces as behavioral and ideological constraints.
- Public/private boundaries: private members, data selves, surveillance, identity, visibility.
- Revision and contingency: systems could be otherwise; history can be rewritten.
- Anti-mythological humor: undercut the philosopher-king, founder, expert, and author before they harden into authority.
- Infrastructure as a repeated medium: shared identity, routing, storage, deployment, and agents recur across very different products.

## Current-store critique

- The visual system contains relevant ingredients—black/white, Iosevka, hot pink, rules, technical labels—but applies them as generic technical minimalism.
- The empty catalog receives more space than the product story.
- The longest and most idiosyncratic brand asset, `somewhatintelligent`, is treated as a normal heading instead of a compositional object.
- The site currently separates content, product, software, and person instead of showing their causality.
- “Wearable field notes” is polished but generic; the shirt’s actual language is much more alive.

## External precedents

- [032c](https://www.032c.com/pages/about) — explicitly operates as media and fashion in one system, supporting the idea that editorial work and garments need not be separate brands.
- [Brain Dead](https://wearebraindead.com/pages/about-us/tag) — describes itself as a catalyst interpreting culture through clothes, objects, and artworks; useful categorical permission for a multi-medium label.
- [Are.na](https://www.are.na/about) and its essay [Information Systems](https://www.are.na/editorial/information-systems) — useful for interfaces organized around collections, connections, and accumulated research rather than a conventional feed.
- [MACK](https://www.mackbooks.us/pages/about-mack) — a model for joining critical dialogue with rigorous material production.

## Research conclusion

The distinctive opportunity is not to synthesize these references visually. It is to combine their mechanisms with Apostoli's recurring contradiction: a serious technical apparatus that keeps betraying the desire and fallibility of the person operating it.
